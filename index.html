### Interpretation: Insight & Policy Decision

#### Key Factors Influencing Career Prospects

1. **Country**: Location significantly impacts salary levels and job opportunities in cybersecurity. Countries with a higher demand for cybersecurity professionals and a higher cost of living tend to offer better salaries. Job seekers should consider relocating to regions with a strong tech industry presence and higher compensation packages.

2. **Expertise Level**: Experience and expertise level are critical determinants of salary. As confirmed by the ANOVA test, individuals with higher expertise levels, such as senior or expert roles, command significantly higher salaries. Job seekers should focus on gaining relevant experience and advancing their skills through continuous learning and certifications.

3. **Company Size**: Larger companies often have more resources and higher budgets for cybersecurity roles, leading to better compensation packages. However, smaller companies and startups may offer unique opportunities for rapid career growth and diverse experience. Job seekers should weigh the benefits of working in different company sizes based on their career goals.

#### Recommendations for Job Seekers

1. **Focus on Gaining Experience**: Prioritize gaining hands-on experience in cybersecurity roles. Internships, entry-level positions, and continuous skill development through certifications can help you move up the expertise levels and achieve higher salaries.

2. **Consider Relocation**: Explore job opportunities in regions with a high demand for cybersecurity professionals. Countries and cities with thriving tech industries often offer better compensation and career growth prospects.

3. **Evaluate Company Size**: Consider the advantages and disadvantages of working in companies of different sizes. Large companies may offer better salaries and job security, while smaller companies can provide diverse experience and faster career progression.

4. **Stay Informed and Network**: Keep up with industry trends, salary benchmarks, and job market demands. Networking with professionals in the field and attending industry events can provide valuable insights and job opportunities.
